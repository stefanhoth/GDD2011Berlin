WTF is GTUG?

GTUGS are user groups for people who are interested in Google’s developer technology; from Android, App Engine to product APIs like the YouTube & the Google+ API.

www.gtugs.org

About the Developer-Weekend:

From the 17th to 20th of November the german GTUGs have organized a series of Web/Mobile/Tech-related events around the Google Developer Day in Berlin. Check out now Schedule

Starting with the Hack&Tell on Thursday, where interested Hackers can present the projects they where working on, we will kickstart you on Friday with our whole day BootCamp about Android, HTML5 and Googel AppEngine. On Sunday, after the GDD, you will have the opportunity to use all your newly gained knowledge to lay hands on new exciting or existing projects together with all your new friends.
